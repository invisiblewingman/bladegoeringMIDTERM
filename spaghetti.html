Simple Spaghetti

Ingredients:  Dillons Price Checker

  • Spaghetti or similar noodles
  • A container of pre‐made sauce
  • Butter
  • Diced Onion
  • 1 lb. beef chuck

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of water and salt moderately
  2. Add a small dollop of butter to a frying pan, then apply pan to heat
  3. Add onions to pan and lightly fry
  4. After caramelization, remove the onions from the pan while frying the beef chuck before seasoning to taste
  5. Boil the pasta before straining
  6. Mix ingredients and serve
