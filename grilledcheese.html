Heavy Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:  Dillons Price Checker

  • Bread of your choice
  • Cheese(s) of your choice (recommended: American, Swiss, and other non‐artisanal cheeses)
  • Butter
  • Diced Onion
  • Deli meat slices
  • Mustard

Cooking Instructions

  1. Turn stovetop to medium‐high heat
  2. Add a small dollop of butter to a frying pan, then apply pan to heat
  3. While the butter melts, spread a small amount of mustard on two pieces of bread
  4. Add onions to pan and stir frequently
  5. As the onions brown, stack the sandwich with your cheese, meat, and any other additives
  6. Once the onions have reached a slight crisp, spoon them out of the pan and onto the sandwich
  7. (If needed, add some extra butter to the pan) Add sandwich to pan; flip after the bottom has browned
  8. Once the sandwich has finished frying, remove it from the heat and serve immediately
